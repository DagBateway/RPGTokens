Paper Tokens Generator

Paper Tokens Generator is a free, premium companion tool designed for Dungeon Masters (DMs) and Tabletop RPG players to instantly generate custom physical miniatures. Print round/square creature tokens, monster initiative tents, and fold-and-cut paper standee pawns for Dungeons & Dragons (D&D), Pathfinder, Fate, Savage Worlds, or any other d20 tabletop game.

Ditch expensive plastic minis! Simply paste any creature portrait URL or upload your files directly from Dropbox, customize your sheet parameters, and print immediately. Our generator automatically fits your designs on an A4 page, saving paper and ink while keeping your d20 battle maps beautiful.

How It Works — Step-by-Step

1. Paste or Upload

Paste a direct image URL of your creature into the link box and click Add Token, or click the Dropbox button to import multiple images at once.

2. Customize Options

Choose your global shape (Round or Square). For each creature, easily adjust its Name, Size (Tiny to Gargantuan), Quantity, starting count, and toggle layout types.

3. Print & Craft

Press Print to save to PDF or send it straight to your printer! You can also download individual high-resolution PNG tokens to use in Virtual Tabletops (VTT).
